The Hathlangoo Premier League (HPL) is a non-professional T10/T20 cricket league played in Hathlangoo Sopore conventionally contested between July and August of every year with 16+ teams representing different villages & Clubs. It is one of the leading leagues in North Kashmir.Thousands of spectators optically perceive the visuals of this fantastic league every year at Habba Sahab Cricket Stadium Hathlangoo.See More

History

The league was founded by the Sports Council Hathlangoo (SCH) in 2009. The aim to launch this League was to truncate the caliber of despondence among incipient generation categorically in Youth. And to curb the expansion of Evil activities in the Society.

Broadcasting & Sponsorship

The HPL is the most-attended cricket league in north Kashmir and became the most watched sporting event to be broadcast live on YouTube and Facebook. In 13th Season of the league, the Eagle Eye Security Solutions and The Fruit Traders of Hathlangoo took the Sponsorship of the Tournament.
